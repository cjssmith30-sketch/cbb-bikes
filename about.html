CBB BIKES WAS BUILT ON THE TRAILS

What started as a one-person fabrication shop in a Durango garage has grown into one of Colorado's most respected custom bike builders. CBB (Colorado Bike Builders) was founded on a simple belief: every rider deserves a bike that fits their body, their terrain, and their ambition.

From hand-welded custom frames to competition-spec race builds, every bike that leaves our shop carries our commitment to craftsmanship and performance. We're riders ourselves — we know what it means to hit the trail at dawn, what it feels like when a bike is truly dialed in.

Today we build, service, and sponsor riders across Colorado and beyond, hosting Bike Week events that bring our community together every season.
